Title: Privacy Policy for Unboxing Reality TikTok API: Effective Date: January 21, 2026 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how Unboxing Reality TikTok API ("we," "our," or "the App") collects and handles information when you use our services to post content to TikTok. We are committed to protecting your privacy. 2. Information We Collect TikTok Authentication: We use official TikTok OAuth protocols to request access to your account. This includes your access token, which is required to post videos. Video Content: We access the video files you intentionally select for upload from your local storage or connected n8n workflow. 3. How We Use Your Data Facilitating Uploads: Your data is used exclusively to facilitate the transfer of video content from your source to TikTok via the official Content Posting API. No Selling of Data: We do not sell your personal information or content to third parties. No AI Training: We do not use your personal data or uploaded content for developing or training AI/ML models. 4. Data Storage and Security As this is an n8n-based automation, your video data is processed through your local n8n instance. We do not store your videos on external servers. Access tokens are stored securely within your n8n credentials. 5. User Rights and Control You can revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok account settings or by deleting your credentials within your n8n instance.
